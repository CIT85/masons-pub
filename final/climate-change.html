Climate Change Animal Impacts

Animal Impact Boxes

Habitat Loss

Rising temperatures and melting ice are destroying natural habitats. Many species — including polar bears, seals, and arctic birds — depend on stable ice and cold environments.

Changing Migration Patterns

Birds, whales, butterflies, and other migratory species depend on predictable seasons. Climate shifts change food availability, causing migration paths to become misaligned with the environment.

Threatened Food Sources

Coral bleaching, droughts, and warming oceans reduce the food supply for countless animals, from sea turtles to tropical fish.
